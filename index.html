Welcome to Mapping Victory!

This project is a digital collection of souvenir maps produced by U.S. Army units at the end of World War II in Europe. These maps tell the story of the war through the eyes of the soldiers who served in it. They provide a window into the ways that Americans experienced the war, the transition to peace, the discovery of Nazi crimes, and their encounter with the people and places of Europe and the Mediterranean.
